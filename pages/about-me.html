ABOUT ME

This is my story and how I came to connect with the world of music. My name is Nathael Bellezza. I was born on December 26, 1994 in the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina. I never really felt a connection to the musical world, I spent my teenage years looking for what I was good at, what I wanted to do and what I was passionate about. I hit the wall many times, in careers and jobs that were for me, that bored me and stressed too much. One day a friend lent me his acoustic guitar. That day, that guitar, was the one that changed everything for me. I got to know a new world, a world in which I could disconnect and let myself flow through my senses. I started playing every day, 8 hours a day. Until one day I felt stuck in what I was learning, so I decided to become a professional, I started studying at a music institute. I started to be interested in singing and other instruments and here I am, developing myself and as a person. Knowing this world was the best thing that happened to me in my life. It was the right decision.
